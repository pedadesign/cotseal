📝 Key Takeaways from this Lesson:

  • 🎵 Young Vietnamese people enjoy photography, reading, and music as healthy entertainment
  • ⚖️ The key phrase: "lựa chọn hình thức giải trí phù hợp với bản thân" — choose entertainment that suits you
  • 📷 Khánh uses photography to restore balance (lấy lại cân bằng) in his life
  • 📚 Kim Hồng says books bring concentration, thinking skills, and a feeling of being yourself
